❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is dental treatment in Chengdu safe?

Yes. Chengdu has many modern, international-standard dental clinics that use sterile techniques, advanced equipment, and globally recognized implant systems like Straumann and Nobel Biocare.

How much can I save on dental implants in China?

Patients typically save 50-70% compared to US, NZ, or Australian prices. A single implant costing $4,000 in New Zealand may cost $1,200-$1,800 in Chengdu.

How long do I need to stay in Chengdu for dental implants?

For implants, most patients require two trips: 3-5 days for placement, and another 4-7 days after 4-6 months for final crowns. Some clinics offer faster solutions like All-on-4 in one extended stay.

Do dentists in Chengdu speak English?

Many international-focused clinics have English-speaking coordinators and dentists. We recommend confirming language support before booking.
