1. Introduction

KIWI Serenity Limited ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to all personal information collected through our coordination services, including cross-border travel arrangements and non-medical logistical coordination relating to third-party partner institutions.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Identity Information: Name, passport/ID number, date of birth, nationality
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, residential address
  • Travel Information: Itinerary details, travel preferences, accommodation requirements
  • Health Information: Health-related information voluntarily provided by you for non-medical coordination and scheduling purposes only, collected only with your explicit consent via our health questionnaire. Providing such information is entirely voluntary.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage our cross-border coordination services (travel arrangements, appointment scheduling with partner institutions)
  • To communicate with you about your itinerary, bookings, and updates
  • To process payments and maintain financial records
  • To comply with legal obligations under New Zealand law
  • To improve our services and customer experience
  • To ensure the security of our services and prevent fraud

Important Note Regarding Medical Information

Any health or medical information collected is used solely for coordination purposes to facilitate scheduling and communication with independent third-party partner institutions in China, at your request and with your consent. We do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. All medical decisions and treatments are between you and the healthcare provider.

We may disclose your personal information in the following circumstances:
