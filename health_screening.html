Services We Can Help Coordinate

We assist with scheduling diagnostic procedures requested by a licensed physician. All medical services are provided by independent partner facilities. Final costs and necessity are determined by the physician.

General Medical Consultation

Assistance in arranging an appointment with a general practitioner at a partner clinic for routine evaluation.
  • Consultation scheduling
  • Basic vital signs recording (by provider)
  • Summary report issued by the clinic
Arranged at partner medical centers

Laboratory Test Coordination

Scheduling assistance for blood and urine analysis at accredited laboratories in Chengdu.
  • Complete blood count (CBC)
  • Basic metabolic & lipid panels
  • Thyroid function tests
Physician order required for each test

Specialist-Requested Lab Tests

Coordination for serum marker tests as ordered by a consulting physician.
  • AFP, CEA, CA19-9, CA125
  • PSA (male health panel)
  • Custom panel per physician's request
Only with a specialist's referral

Abdominal Ultrasound Arrangement

Scheduling for ultrasound examination of abdominal organs.
  • Liver, gallbladder, pancreas
  • Spleen and both kidneys
  • Timing depends on provider availability
Non‑invasive procedure

Thyroid Ultrasound Coordination

Assistance booking a sonographic examination of the thyroid gland.
  • Full thyroid imaging
  • Doppler vascular assessment (if ordered)
  • Report provided by the medical institution
Physician order required

Cardiac Testing Coordination

Assistance with scheduling non‑invasive cardiac examinations.
  • Resting electrocardiogram (ECG)
  • Transthoracic echocardiogram
  • Report interpreted by the medical provider
Additional services may be discussed directly with the provider if required

MRI Appointment Assistance

Assistance coordinating imaging appointments requested by the medical provider.
  • Brain, spine, joints, or abdomen
  • Contrast media when prescribed
  • Digital images and radiologist report
Requires physician order for specific region

CT Scan Arrangement

Coordination for computed tomography of head, sinuses, abdomen, pelvis, or extremities.
  • Head / sinuses / spine CT
  • Abdominopelvic CT
  • IV contrast if prescribed
Per provider's imaging protocol

Endoscopy Coordination

Assistance arranging upper GI endoscopy or colonoscopy under sedation at partner facilities.
  • Gastroscopy (upper GI)
  • Colonoscopy (lower GI)
  • Biopsy / polypectomy (billed separately)
Pre-procedure consultation is conducted by the medical provider

Bone Density Scanning

Schedule DEXA scans for bone mineral density measurement.
  • Lumbar spine and hip scans
  • Quick, low‑radiation procedure
  • Report issued directly by the medical institution
Physician order required

Gender‑Specific Health Coordination

Arranging pelvic ultrasound, mammography, testicular ultrasound, or hormone panel tests as ordered.
  • Pelvic ultrasound / Mammography
  • Testicular ultrasound / PSA
  • Hormone level tests
Physician order required for each service

PET‑CT Coordination

Assistance with whole‑body PET‑CT scanning at specialized nuclear medicine centers.
  • Full‑body PET‑CT
  • Nuclear medicine specialist report
  • Referral from attending physician required
Only upon specialist referral
