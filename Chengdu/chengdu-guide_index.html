🐼 Chengdu at a Glance

Chengdu, capital of Sichuan province, is a vibrant city where ancient traditions meet modern development. Known for giant pandas, spicy cuisine, and a famously relaxed lifestyle, it's the perfect destination for medical travelers seeking both treatment and cultural enrichment.
