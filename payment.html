Pre-Trip Preparation
1. 开通国际漫游或购买中国SIM卡/eSIM（确保网络连接）
Enable international roaming or buy China SIM/eSIM (ensure internet access)
2. 下载支付宝/微信，绑定国际信用卡（Visa/Mastercard/Amex）
Download Alipay/WeChat, link international credit cards (Visa/Mastercard/Amex)
3. 准备少量人民币现金（500-1000元）用于小额或应急支付
Prepare small amount of RMB cash (500-1000 yuan) for small or emergency payments

2026 new: Alipay "TourCard" designed for international visitors, top up and use directly

Payment Methods Details
支付宝 (Alipay) – 支持绑定国际卡，单笔限额已提升至500元
Alipay – supports linking int'l cards, single transaction limit raised to 500 yuan
微信支付 (WeChat Pay) – “钱包”中添加国际银行卡，覆盖面更广
WeChat Pay – add int'l bank card in "Wallet", wider acceptance
国际信用卡 – 2026年更多商户支持非接触支付，酒店/大型商场普遍接受
Int'l credit cards – more contactless payment options in 2026, accepted at hotels & large malls
现金人民币 – 传统市场、小店、备用方案，建议使用新版纸币
Cash RMB – traditional markets, small shops, backup solution; use new series notes
Everyday Payment Scenarios
🍽️ 餐厅用餐 – 扫描桌码点餐支付，更多餐厅提供双语菜单
Dining – scan table QR code; more restaurants offer bilingual menus
🚕 出租车/网约车 – 扫描司机二维码或App内支付，滴滴支持外卡
Taxi/ride-hailing – scan driver's QR or in-app; DiDi supports foreign cards
🚇 地铁公交 – 支付宝/微信“乘车码”，覆盖主要城市
Subway/bus – "Ride Code" in Alipay/WeChat, covers major cities
🏨 酒店住宿 – 国际卡预授权 + 移动支付结算，更多自助入住
Hotel – int'l card pre-auth + mobile payment; more self-check-in options
Key Tips & Recommendations
📶 稳定网络连接 – 移动支付依赖网络，建议购买本地流量包
Stable internet – mobile payments require network; buy local data package
🔄 支付失败时尝试切换支付宝/微信，或检查网络
If payment fails, switch between Alipay/WeChat or check connection
💾 保存支付截图 – 便于查询或争议，建议保留24小时
Save payment screenshots – for reference or disputes, keep for 24h
⚠️ 避免公共Wi-Fi支付 – 使用移动数据或可信网络
Avoid public Wi-Fi for payments – use mobile data or trusted network
