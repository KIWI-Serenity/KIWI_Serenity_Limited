📘 Article 2 · Implant Guide

Dental Implants in China: What International Patients Should Know

Affordable dental implants ChinaDental implants China

What Is a Dental Implant?

A titanium post surgically placed into the jawbone, providing a stable foundation for crowns, bridges, or full arch restorations.

Typical Process

  • Initial Consultation with digital scans
  • Implant Placement under local anesthesia
  • Healing Period (osseointegration): 3–6 months
  • Final Restoration with custom crown

Why Patients Choose China

  • Lower treatment costs
  • Modern technology: 3D CBCT, guided surgery
  • Highly trained specialists
